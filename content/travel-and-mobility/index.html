Travel and mobility

Getnet for Travel and Mobility

A global payment solution designed for travel agencies, e-mobility companies and more to meet customer needs.

Benefits Overview Risk and security Features

Travel pack

Customer-centric merchants must ensure end-to-end journeys are close to perfection. The travel payment process is a key opportunity to create an optimal experience for the user.

Improve user experience

Getnet payment pages deliver a highly customized, secure checkout experience so you can give your customers the experience they deserve and expect.

Accept payments from everywhere

Let your customers pay how they want to pay. Support cards, bank redirects and direct debits, invoice now and pay later payment methods and more.

Maximize security and compliance

Getnet Fraud Prevention solution helps you to detect and block illegitimate transaction attempts. Stop blocking genuine shoppers right now.

Simplify your payment ecosystem

On the comprehensive visual dashboard, get detailed statistics on cards and alternative payment methods. Search for specific criteria, such as the status of transactions, merchant account information, chargebacks, refunds, and more.

Service from a single source

Lasting success in the tourism industry also depends on offering customers sufficient choice of national and international payment methods.

Our portfolio includes acceptance contracts for almost all credit cards as well as a number of alternative payment methods.

This enables you to accept credit card payments from virtually all major organisations: easily securely and at low cost. Thus improving your competitiveness, expanding your customer base and tapping into sales potential worldwide.

See payment methods

One platform

Getnet´s platform enables you to accept international multi-currency payments from all major card schemes and numerous alternative payment methods. And it also offers you international cross channel reconciliation, and multi-currency settlement options. Check your payment settlements and reconciliation on the platform and get reports of all available channels.

Multichannel solutions

Getnet supports multi-channel sales for all business models in the tourism industry via one payment platform. As a rule, customers with complex systems have a single point of contact with us, no matter which sector you are in. Our single integration payment platform makes it possible for you to carry out multi-channel sales, ranging from E-Commerce, M-Commerce, mail order and call centers. We ensure the end-to-end process in your value creation is maximized.

Risk management and security

We offer intelligent fraud recognition and fraud protection systems that minimize the risk of attempted fraud across all payment methods.

Our risk management platform effectively prevents fraud for national and international card schemes and alternative payment methods.

Our modular solutions can be integrated simply and easily into your existing payment ecosystem.
